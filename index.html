Welcome to Olena's Website

Olena was originally from Ukraine, but had moved to Texas in Houston in 2018. Olena is a highly motivated individual, who always had a passion for technology and programming. She had taught herself how to code, and after years of hard work, she finally became an expert in web development.

In her free time, Olena enjoyed reading books. She was also a big fan of hiking and exploring nature. She likes to take her 10 lb dog, a little dachshund named Major, on long walks and adventures. Olena and Major are inseparable, and they spent a lot of time together.

Olena is happily married to her husband, Alex. Together, they had started a YouTube channel. Their channel now became a platform for them to share their travel adventures around the world. Olena and Alex posted videos of their travels to different countries, sharing their experiences, recommendations, and tips for travelers.
